Doctor Who: An Adventure Of Time & Space

Doctor Who First Aired On The BBC 23 November 1963 It's the longest running and most famous sci fi show on earth
The Daleks are the name of the enemy of the Doctor They are an alien race built on hate
The Cybermen are another well known enemy of the Doctor Unlike the daleks, they are metal men or cyborgs
The Doctor has had many faces, he regenerates He is currently on his 14th regeneration
He has been portrayed by 13 different actors, one for each 'regeneration' The latest actor to be playing the Doctor is Peter Capaldi
